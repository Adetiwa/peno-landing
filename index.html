FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Peno and how it works.

  • Will my customers know they're talking to AI?

    Peno sounds completely natural. We train it on real Nigerian conversations so it speaks like your customers do — whether that's formal English, Pidgin, or local slang. Customers feel heard, not automated.

  • Is it hard to set up?

    Not at all. Scan a QR code, upload your products (Excel or PDF works fine), and you're live in under 5 minutes. No tech skills needed — if you can use WhatsApp, you can use Peno.

  • What if Peno doesn't know the answer?

    Peno alerts you instantly when it needs help. You can jump in anytime, and Peno will step back the moment you start typing. You're always in control of your customer relationships.

  • Will my WhatsApp get banned?

    No. Peno operates within WhatsApp's terms by mimicking natural human response times and behaviour. We've helped hundreds of businesses stay safe while automating their DMs successfully.
